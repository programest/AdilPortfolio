Introduction

About Work

I made this site with classmates for the  Almaty bike rental . We did practice there. If you want to know what it is, here here is the information.

In this project, I was  Tim the leader, we had a team of 3 people, someone did the design, someone you did the icons, someone made up, I made up and controlled the whole process, gave tasks and showed it to the management, It was very interesting  and a little difficult. Being a Team Leader is very difficult.



We have been doing this project for a little over 2 months. But unfortunately, we  did not finish it  because of the end of the practice, very sad, but I hope we will finish this.

I think I made this site on the 9.5 out of 10

Process

We started with the fact that we need to make a header, we decided to make it in 2 rows, since there are many sections. It was decided to make a swiper in order to publish news. A counter of numbers was also used to attract attention.

AlmatyBike had problems with the fact that people did not understand how to take a bike and this block was the most important he explained to people how and where to press to take it. I think this block is more than perfect

The main functionality in the mobile application, therefore, it is necessary to make a block about this on the site.

There are only 3 bike rentals in Kazakhstan, we reviewed their sites when creating our own, and of course, we need it to be different from them. I came up with and made this block. It shows the advantages of our bike.

I thought that it would be better to make the news in a separate block and with the scrolling, the new news will be in the first two, the rest are next.

I really like this block and the animation is block FAQ

I first created my QR code when switching to it, depending on the OС of the phone IOS or ANDROID, then it will open the play market if android and if IOS then app store
