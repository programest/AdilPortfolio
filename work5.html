Introduction

About Work

We did this project for a  competition in college. It was necessary to complete the project in just 24 hours, with design, frontend and upload it to the hosting .

It's the first time I've done a project so fast . The theme was "What good would you do for the world" . We decided to link our site with the theme of sports. The theme was that teenagers would do more sports and it would be easier for them to do it with the help of our site. Here you can see where there are  sports fields .



In the first 5 hours, we sketched out the design and decided on the theme. I had to work at night . The rest of the time I made up and uploaded to the hosting. And the partner completed the design. It was very difficult .

I think I did it on 6 out of 10.

Process

We had to create a MainColor and we made a great gradient and we used it. It was great. It was necessary to create a colorful main and it turned out!

Next, a block about who we are, here you need to very accurately describe why we are needed and what advantages we have

We thought for a long time how to place the most important block, because there was a lot of text. It was decided that it would be in the form of blocks with links

We also decided that we need to divide the information into sections. What would be convenient for the user

Comments are a very important part of every website.

In the most important information, it is important to describe every detail, price, location, and so on so that the user does not have any questions
