Introduction

About Work

I made this project for my  use. This app has two modes:  "Game" and  "Work" and it allows me to switch between them either by tapping or by voice.

 "Game": Turns off additional screens and switches sound to headphones

 "Work" Turns on all screens and switches sound to speakers

This project can be improved and make a good  voice assistant   that will

I think I did it on 8 out of 10.

Process

Since I need an application that will always be active and working in the application does not suit me, I chose: Electron

For speech recognition I chose Whisper. Yes, it could have been done with faster solutions, but for my project this is enough.

In order for the program to understand the voice, it records audio for 5 seconds and Whisper deciphers it
