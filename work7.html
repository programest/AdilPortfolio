Introduction

About Work

This is my first serious project at Interteach. There I worked as an intern and was a Frontend developer. The travel insurance website hadn't been updated for a long time and I needed to fix it.

The most difficult thing here is the type of insurance. There is multiple and there issingle , there was a lot of work with numbers and coefficients. I did the design myself and finalized the interface , and it was ready to use .



In addition, I needed to implement a personal account and login forms and "forgot the password".All this was very good practice for me. There were a lot of problems but when it was in prod it was  very cool.

I think I did it on 8 out of 10.

Process

The project required multilingualism. I made it with Google Translate and Local Translation.

There are a lot of travel insurance sites and we need a block with USO that will attract users

Not everyone understands why you need insurance. And when we got a call to CallCenter with this question, it was decided to post it here

Our insurance company is officially working on this documents, this is important

It was decided to make the entrance to the personal account in the style of the entire page and it is quite convenient

In the personal account itself there is a brief information about the user and his policies . And all the information you need

Complex Block

Difficult: There were many difficulties in the form itself. It was necessary to make it possible to select a country, and it was necessary to make your pack with the display of flags and the correct display of the country name. It was necessary to collect an array with data and send to the server and, depending on the result, check a lot of configurations.

Decision: I decided by reading the documentation and ChatGPT and my colleague Backend developer helped a lot.
